Instructions

Throughout the entire experiment you will only need the keyboard. You will first read a story. Press the SPACEBAR once you have read and understood it. To select one of the pictures, you will need to remember details of the story, so make sure you read it carefully!

You will then see the three images. You will be instructed to select one of the images that relates to the story.

Press F to select the Picture on the left. Press J to select the Picture on the right.

Press B to select the red X.

With response efficiency in mind, we would like for you to keep your hands poised above the keys as shown in the picture below. It is very important to maintain this position throughout the experiment! Press the SPACEBAR to continue.
