As a reminder:

You will first read a story. Press the SPACEBAR once you have read and understood it.

You will then see two images and one red X. You will be instructed to select one of the images. Your job is to pick the image that best fits the instruction.

Press F to select the Picture on the left. Press J to select the Picture on the right.

Press B to select the red X, if neither of the images is appropriate.

Before the Experiment begins, you will go through two practice trials in order to get the hang of the task.

After this, we will begin the actual experiment, which should last between 5 and 10 minutes.
