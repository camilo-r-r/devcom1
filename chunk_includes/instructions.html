Instructions

In this experiment, You will read a series of short stories about everyday life.

At the end of each story you will see two images that may depict the content of the story, and one red X.

Together with these images you will read a sentence instructing you to select one of the images.

Your task is to select the picture that best fits the instruction given.

If neither of the two images is appropriate, please choose the red X instead.

It is very important that you stay focused on the task throughout the experiment and that you try to complete the experiment in one sitting.
